Chilli Export from Kenya to Europe: A Growing Opportunity

Kenya’s climate is ideal for growing red chillies, and the demand in European markets is steadily increasing. At PCI AgroExport, we cultivate high-yield varieties like Bird’s Eye and Cayenne, using sustainable farming practices. Chilli exports offer great income potential for local farmers, with our company handling certification, sorting, and cold chain logistics. Europe values Kenya’s spicy, organic produce—and we are proud to be part of this green export revolution.

Strawberry Farming & Exports: Kenya’s Sweet Potential

Strawberry farming in Kenya has transformed rural livelihoods, especially in regions like Laikipia and Limuru. PCI AgroExport has introduced precision farming, mulching, and controlled irrigation, enabling consistent production. Our strawberries are exported to the Gulf and EU markets, where freshness and sweetness are prized. We work with contract farmers to ensure traceability and food safety while supporting local youth and women in agribusiness.
