About Our Rumuruti Farm

Nestled in the fertile highlands of Laikipia County, our 200-acre smart farm in Rumuruti combines traditional agricultural wisdom with cutting-edge AI technology to produce exceptional export-quality crops.

Precision Agriculture

Our farm utilizes:

  • AI-powered irrigation systems
  • Drone crop monitoring
  • Soil sensors for optimal nutrition
  • Automated climate control greenhouses

Directions to Our Farm

From Nairobi:

  1. Take A2 highway north toward Nyeri (approx. 150km)
  2. At Nyeri, take B5 toward Rumuruti (approx. 80km)
  3. Look for our signage 5km past Rumuruti town

GPS Coordinates: 0.2684° N, 36.5389° E

Visits by appointment only. Please contact us in advance.

Our Premium Crops

Grown in the ideal climate of Laikipia County with elevation of 1,800m above sea level, our crops benefit from:

  • Rich volcanic soils
  • Consistent sunlight
  • Pure mountain water irrigation
  • Precision farming techniques

Strawberries

Sweet, juicy strawberries grown in our climate-controlled greenhouses. Year-round production with consistent quality.

Varieties: Chandler, San Andreas

Harvest: Year-round

Chilies

High-capsaicin Bird's Eye and Habanero varieties. Sun-dried or fresh, perfect for global spice markets.

Varieties: Bird's Eye, Habanero

Scoville: 50,000-350,000 SHU

French Beans

Tender, crisp beans meeting strict EU export standards. Grown with minimal pesticide use.

Varieties: Samantha, Paulista

Length: 12-15cm

Tomatoes

Firm, flavorful tomatoes for both fresh market and processing. Grown in our hydroponic systems.

Varieties: Anna F1, Tylka F1

Brix: 5.5-6.5%

Onions

Sweet, pungent onions with excellent shelf life. Ideal for Middle Eastern and European markets.

Varieties: Red Creole, Bombay Red

Size: 50-70mm diameter

Snow Peas

Sweet, flat-podded peas with tender edible pods. Quick-growing and high-yielding.

Varieties: Oregon Sugar Pod, Mammoth Melting

Pod Length: 7-10cm
