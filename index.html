Why Choose Public Choice Investment?

Public Choice Investment (PCI) stands at the forefront of Kenya's agricultural export industry. With deep roots in sustainable farming, PCI offers a unique combination of tradition and innovation. Our produce—ranging from fresh strawberries and chilies to French beans and ravaya—is cultivated using modern agritech methods ensuring high yield, exceptional quality, and compliance with EU export standards.

PCI partners with local farmers to promote fair trade, supports eco-friendly practices that conserve soil and water, and ensures traceability across the entire supply chain. With direct market access to Europe and the Middle East, we eliminate unnecessary middlemen, delivering better value to farmers and buyers alike.

Choosing PCI means choosing consistency, quality assurance, and a partner committed to food safety and social impact. From greenhouse to global shelf, PCI guarantees freshness, transparency, and trust.
